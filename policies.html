5. Refunds, withdrawal and cancellations

Because FitBrand Starter Plan is a digital product, refunds are generally not offered after the product has been accessed, downloaded or delivered, unless required by mandatory consumer law, there is a technical access issue we cannot fix, or the product is materially not delivered.

If you have an access problem, email support@fitbrand.fit with your order email and receipt. We will try to resolve the issue before any refund decision.

6. Health, training and nutrition disclaimer

FitBrand content is general information only. It is not medical advice and does not replace advice from a doctor, physiotherapist, dietitian, personal trainer or other qualified professional.

You are responsible for deciding whether exercises, meal ideas, calorie estimates, macros, training volume, intensity and lifestyle suggestions are appropriate for you. Stop exercising if you feel pain, dizziness, chest discomfort, faintness or unusual symptoms. Seek professional help before starting a new program if you are under 18, pregnant, injured, ill, taking medication, have a medical condition, have an eating disorder history or have special dietary needs.

Results are not guaranteed. Results depend on genetics, effort, consistency, sleep, nutrition, adherence, experience, stress, health and other individual factors.

FitBrand generators are educational tools that create general suggestions based on user inputs. They may contain mistakes, may not account for your full situation and should be reviewed with common sense. Outputs should be adapted to your ability, equipment, recovery and safety.

The generators do not diagnose, treat, prevent or cure any condition. They do not replace professional coaching, medical treatment or qualified nutritional guidance.

FitBrand is intended for users aged 13+. Users aged 13–17 may only use FitBrand with parent or guardian permission. Users under 18 should involve a responsible adult before buying, training, following nutrition guidance or using any fitness plan.
