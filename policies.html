One-time purchase Policy

FitBrand Starter Plan is currently tested as a one-time digital purchase through Gumroad. One-time purchase products are not active unless clearly shown at checkout before payment.

  • One-time purchases renew automatically every month until cancelled.
  • The customer authorises FitBrand and its payment provider to charge the saved payment method on each renewal date.
  • Customers can cancel future renewals by contacting support@fitbrand.fit or by using account/billing tools when available.
  • Cancellation stops future renewals; it does not automatically refund previous paid periods unless required by law or stated in a specific offer.
  • Access normally continues until the end of the paid billing period after cancellation.
  • If payment fails, access may pause, downgrade or cancel until the payment issue is fixed.
  • FitBrand may change one-time purchase features or pricing. Customers will be informed where required before a material change affects them.

Refund and cancellation rights depend on product type, delivery status, one-time purchase status and mandatory consumer law.

  • For digital content and digital services, customers may be asked to consent to immediate access and acknowledge that withdrawal rights may be lost once delivery/access begins.
  • If a customer has not accessed a digital product or if mandatory law gives a right to withdraw, FitBrand will handle the request according to applicable consumer rules.

  • Refunds are not normally given for lack of results, not using the content, forgetting to cancel a one-time purchase, or personal preference after access has been delivered, unless required by law.

Health, Training & Nutrition Disclaimer

FitBrand content is general fitness and nutrition information. It is not medical advice, diagnosis, treatment or a replacement for a doctor, physiotherapist, dietitian or other qualified professional.

  • Speak to a healthcare professional before starting if you have injuries, medical conditions, medication use, pregnancy, eating disorder history or other concerns.

  • Meal plans, calories and macros are estimates and may not fit every person.
  • FitBrand does not guarantee weight loss, muscle gain, strength results, health outcomes or any specific transformation.

AI Tool Policy

FitBrand AI tools generate plans based on customer inputs. AI output can be useful, but it can be incomplete, unsuitable or inaccurate if the inputs are wrong or if the user has special needs.

  • Customers must enter accurate information, including age, weight, height, goal, allergies, limitations and training level.
  • AI outputs must be reviewed by the customer before use and adjusted for injuries, equipment, recovery, schedule and health status.
  • Do not follow AI meal guidance that conflicts with allergies, medical guidance, religious/dietary restrictions or personal safety.
  • FitBrand may update, limit, improve, remove or change AI features at any time.

Age, Minors & Parent Permission

FitBrand is not intended for children under 13. Users aged 13–17 may only use FitBrand with permission and supervision from a parent or legal guardian.

  • 13+ users need parent/guardian permission to create an account, use FitBrand tools or purchase one-time purchases/products.
  • Parents/guardians are responsible for checking whether FitBrand content is suitable for the minor.
  • Where privacy or consumer law requires parent/guardian consent for a minor’s personal data or purchases, the parent/guardian must provide that consent.
  • FitBrand may refuse or remove access if age, permission or safety concerns are unclear.
  • AI fitness/nutrition tools should be used with extra caution by minors and should not be used to create extreme dieting or unsafe training plans.
